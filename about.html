My Story ✨

Once upon a time, my humans were on a magical trip to Korea when they stumbled upon the pet store they had been dreaming about for months. The moment they held me in their arms, it was love at first sight! 💖

🐾 Born on October 10, 2024, I’ve been spreading joy and cuddles ever since.

My Favorite Things 🧸
